FAQ

Questions, answered

Do I need to know how to code?

No. Workflows are built visually by connecting steps on a canvas. Advanced users can drop into custom scripts for edge cases, but it's optional.

What happens when a workflow hits something it can't decide?

It pauses and routes the decision to the person you've assigned, with full context attached, so nothing gets automated blindly.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Plans are month-to-month with no contracts, and you can downgrade to Starter at any time without losing your workflows.

Is my data secure?

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Enterprise plans add SSO, audit exports, and custom data retention policies.
